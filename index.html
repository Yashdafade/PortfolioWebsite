About Me

I'm Yash Dafade, a Full Stack Web Developer with a strong background in MERN Stack. I blend technical expertise with a passion for creating impactful web solutions. My focus is on developing responsive and user-friendly applications, leveraging both frontend and backend technologies to deliver high-quality results.
I specialize in ReactJS, Node.js, Express.js, and MySQL, and am skilled in CI/CD practices. My projects include Schoolix, a school management system live at client school,which involves comprehensive pre-admission console, Inventory Management,bill generation console and library management system, currently working on transport management module. I’m dedicated to expanding my knowledge in cloud computing, microservices, and Python for AI and ML, and I’m always eager to embrace new challenges and technologies.
